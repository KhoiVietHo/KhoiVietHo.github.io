Covid-19 Live Updates

Overall

At present, people around the globe are more familiar to Covid-19 than before. Therefore, people are more concerned about how Covid-19 affects their own community
This project used live updated data extracted from website to build the reports, focusing on the Recovering Rate and Risk to Population.

Risk to Population

Covid-19 has put nearly the whole world under risk to population health. 119 countries at very hight risk rate and 103 others at medium risk. With the threshold set at 100k caes/ 1M population.

China stat?

The number suggested that China is in low risk state with 170 caes/1M population. Let's leave the question opened.

Deaths and Recovery Prospect

It shows that the USA was hammered by the pandemic with the most cases and also 16% of them were dead.
However, there are some good news is that most of the countries in the world have very high recovery rate. It means we can eventually deal with this virus as a simple flu.
On the other hand, countries in Europe and Africa seem suffer the virus longer and harder than others.
