Adventure Works

Overall

The project was done using advanced Excel and Power BI. The main purpose of the report is to analyse the sales reality versus target over a period of a bicycle and accessories manufacturer.
The sales' result did not meet the target budget but the detail analysis suggested many more fields and time to improve in the future.

June and August

Total Sales started to spike in June and even exceeded target but fell under target in August. The best seller product is bike. This makes sense because it's summer time.
What could have been done better? Promotion campaign from May - August? Push more sales for bikes?

New Strategy?

Should we push more for Mountain Bikes? Do people really like road bike than we expected?
Cleaners and Fenders in Accessories are doing better than others. And surprisingly, December brings back the highest revenue of the year.
Our fancy customers are in Australia and SounthWest USA, and they do like Mountain Bike-200. What can we do to encourage them to buy more?
